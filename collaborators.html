I am currently a lecture in the school of computer and software, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. I am also working as a researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University, supervised by Prof. Alex X. Liu. I studied PhD degree (2013~2016) in the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology at Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Wanchun Dou. I work as the reviewer of IEEE Transactions on Services Computing, IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing, IEEE Transactions on Big Data, Future Generation Computer System, Software: Practice and Experience, Joural of Parallel and Distributed Computing, Concurrency and Computation: Practice and Experience, Wireless Communications and Mobile Computing, Security and Communication Network, IEEE Access and Joural Of Software. My research interest includes Cloud Computing, Big Data, IoT and Fog Computing, Deep Learning. My Chinese CV could be found here.
