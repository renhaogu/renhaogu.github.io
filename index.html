I am currently a lecture in the school of computer and software, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. I am also working as a researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University, supervised by Prof. Alex X. Liu. I studied PhD degree (2013~2016) in the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology at Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Wanchun Dou. My research interest includes Cloud Computing, Big Data, IoT and Fog Computing, Deep Learning. My Chinese CV could be found here.
